Bankruptcy Dismissal Rates by Federal District

Chapter 7 and Chapter 13 dismissal rates for every major federal bankruptcy district, drawn directly from the Federal Judicial Center Integrated Database.

Home›Districts

Every federal bankruptcy district operates under the same Bankruptcy Code, but dismissal rates vary by more than 20 percentage points from one district to another. Local trustee practices, judge preferences, and attorney competence all affect how likely a case is to be dismissed before discharge.

Each page below shows the actual dismissal rate for Chapter 7 and Chapter 13 in that district, broken down by year, by reason, and compared to the national benchmark. All figures come from the FJC Integrated Database - the public dataset of every federal bankruptcy case disposition.
